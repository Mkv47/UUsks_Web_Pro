About Event:

The Artificial Intelligence Club aims to foster a community of AI enthusiasts, researchers, and learners who are passionate about exploring and advancing the field of artificial intelligence. Our mission is to create a collaborative environment where members can exchange ideas, engage in hands-on projects, and stay updated with the latest developments in AI technology.
