About Event:

- The Iftar Program is a special event held during the month of Ramadan, observed by Muslims worldwide. It marks the breaking of the fast at sunset, a time of unity, spirituality, and community gathering.

- The Iftar Program is hosted at the Üsküdar Municipality Hall, providing a spacious and welcoming environment for attendees.
